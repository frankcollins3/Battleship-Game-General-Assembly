How to play

  • Battleships is a game of skill against the computer.
  • Place your ships on the grid by choosing either horizontal or vertical.
  • Once you have placed all of your ships, click on a square on the computer grid to start playing.
  • The computer will automatically guess each time.
  • The first to destroy all of the opponents ships is the winner.
