How to play

  1. Choose horizontal or vertical and then choose the ship:
    • 1 x Aircraft carrier (5 squares)
    • 1 x Battleship (4 squares)
    • 1 x Cruiser (3 squares)
    • 2 x Destroyer (2 squares)
    • 2 x Submarine (1 square)
  2. Place your ships on the grid by choosing either horizontal or vertical. IMPORTANT! There must be at least one square between each ship, shown by an exclusion zone around the ship.
  3. Once you have placed all of your ships, click on a square on the computer grid to start playing.
  4. The computer will automatically guess each time.
  5. The first to destroy all of the opponent's ships is the winner.
